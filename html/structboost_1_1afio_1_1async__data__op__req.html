Detailed Description

template<class T>
struct boost::afio::async_data_op_req< T >

A convenience bundle of precondition, data and where for reading into a T as specified by its to_asio_buffers() overload. Data MUST stay around until the operation completes.

Template Parameters:
TAny readable (if const) or writable (if non-const) type T as specified by its to_asio_buffers() overload.
